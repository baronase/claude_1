📖 How to Play

Goal: Collect both apples! 🍎

Rules:
• Only move one monkey at a time
• Can only move the top monkey from a stack
• A smaller monkey can only go on top of a larger monkey
• When all monkeys are properly stacked on a peg with an apple, it's consumed!

Strategy: Stack all monkeys on peg 2 to collect the first apple, then move them all to peg 3 for the second apple!
